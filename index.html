Colores con Sentido

Esta herramienta genera paletas de colores suaves y calmantes, seleccionadas para favorecer la percepción visual y el confort en personas con autismo, basándose en estudios científicos sobre el impacto del color en el bienestar sensorial.

Referencias bibliográficas

  1. Grandin, T. (2006). The way I see it: A personal look at autism and Asperger's. Future Horizons.
  2. Happe, F., & Frith, U. (2020). Annual Research Review: Looking back to look forward – changes in the concept of autism and implications for future research. Journal of Child Psychology and Psychiatry, 61(3), 218–232.
  3. Falkmer, M., Anderson, K., Joosten, A., & Falkmer, T. (2013). Diagnostic procedures in autism spectrum disorders: A systematic literature review. European Child & Adolescent Psychiatry, 22(6), 329-340.
  4. Wilbarger, P., & Wilbarger, J. (1991). Defensive sensory processing disorder: A conceptualization and model for treatment. Sensory Integration Special Interest Section Quarterly.
  5. Ben-Sasson, A., Carter, A. S., & Briggs-Gowan, M. J. (2009). Sensory over-responsivity in elementary school: Prevalence and social-emotional correlates. Journal of Abnormal Child Psychology, 37(5), 705-716.
